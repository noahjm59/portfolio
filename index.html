Noah Martin - About

Overview

Noah is a current junior at the University of Michigan studying Information Analysis. He is from Los Angeles, CA, but has lived in four other states including New York, Georgia, Nevada, and Michigan.

Michigan

Since coming to the University of Michigan in August 2018, Noah has been a fan of Michigan sports. Like many other Michigan students, some of the best memories at school have come from a Michigan gameday. He has grown to love Ann Arbor, and can't wait for a more normal senior year at UofM.

LA

Noah moved to Los Angeles from Albany, New York, in August 2015 after his freshman year of high school, moving to the Sherman Oaks neighborhood of Los Angeles. While in school at Michigan, his family moved to the Marina del Rey neighborhood of Los Angeles in order to live closer to the ocean. While he loves living in Los Angeles, and plans on moving back there after graduating from Michigan, he still roots for the New York sports teams.
